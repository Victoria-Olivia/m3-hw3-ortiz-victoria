Who Was Leonardo DaVinci?

The Artist Genius Ahead of His Time

Leonardo DaVinci was an Italian artist, scientist, and inventor. He was born in 1452 in Vinci, Italy. He is best known for his painting the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper. He was a true Renaissance.
